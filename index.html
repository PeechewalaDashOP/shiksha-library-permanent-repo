About Us

About Shiksha Library

Shiksha Library is Kota's premium comfortable study space, purpose-built to help students achieve their academic and competitive exam goals. Nestled in the heart of Kota's student hub, we provide a serene, fully-equipped environment where every detail — from ergonomic seating to silent zones — is designed with the serious aspirant in mind.

Whether you're preparing for IIT JEE, NEET, UPSC, SSC, Banking, or Board exams, our library offers the perfect blend of comfort, discipline, and resources to help you stay focused and consistent. We believe every student deserves a world-class study space — at a price that doesn't break the bank.

  • Safe, secure, CCTV-monitored premises
  • Open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week
  • Conveniently located in Vigyan Nagar, Kota
  • Trusted by 1000+ students and growing
